Hacksaw Ridge

Details

The film focuses on the World War II experiences of Desmond Doss,
an American pacifist combat medic who, as a Seventh-day Adventist Christian,
refused to carry or use a weapon or firearm of any kind.
Doss became the first conscientious objector to be awarded the Medal of Honor,
for service above and beyond the call of duty during the Battle of Okinawa.
