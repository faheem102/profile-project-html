Braveheart

Details

Braveheart is a 1995 American epic historical drama film directed by,
produced by, and starring Mel Gibson. Gibson portrays Sir William Wallace,
a late-13th century Scottish warrior who led the Scots in the
First War of Scottish Independence against King Edward I of England.
