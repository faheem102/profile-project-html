Catch me if you can

Details

The screenplay by Jeff Nathanson is based on the semi-autobiographical
book of the same name by Frank Abagnale Jr., who claims that before his
19th birthday, he successfully performed cons worth millions of dollars
by posing as a Pan American World Airways pilot, a Georgia doctor, and a
Louisiana parish prosecutor. The historical truth of his story is
heavily disputed.
